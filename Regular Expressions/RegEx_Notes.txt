----summary----:

[blabla] : outputs single character
blabla   : looks for exactly blabla

-----rules-----:

[abc] : either a either b either c

[^abc]: neither a, neither b nor c

[a - z] : a single char from a-z

a+ : [1,inf) times a
a* : [0,inf) times a
